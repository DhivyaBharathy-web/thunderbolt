Welcome to Thunderbolt - Seamless Data Migration

Purpose and Vision

Thunderbolt's vision is to offer a comprehensive solution for the hospitality industry, enabling secure and efficient migration of property, reservations, and customer data from legacy systems to modern PMS platforms. Utilizing advanced AI, Thunderbolt anonymizes and stages data for migration while accurately mapping source datasets. It is designed to ensure smooth transitions between PMS systems, emphasizing data accuracy, compliance with privacy standards, and operational continuity.
